About Me

My name is Joseph 'Lance' Easley. I am a graduate from Base Camp Coding Academy and I now work at CoreLogic. My focus recently (largely becuase of work) has been in full-stack development, specifically using the .NET Framework. Personally, I enjoy coding with Python, which comprises a majority of my projects on GitHub.

For the longest time, I have always enjoyed seeing how things work and recreating them to the best of my ability, and budget. The process of a project evolving from a vision to the final product is such an exciting and satisfying journey. Whether such a project is physical or software-based, the development cycle is something I greatly enjoy to this day.

I am currently most interested in back-end/software development. The logic side of an application fascinates me. However, a back-end is mostly useless without a proper UI. I've grown this skill tremendously through my work with CoreLogic, where the UI is often left to dev descretion.
